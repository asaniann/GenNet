# User Guide

## Overview

GenNet Personalized Health Platform provides comprehensive health analysis and predictions.

## For Patients

### Getting Started

1. **Create Account**: Register for an account
2. **Upload Data**: Upload genomic, expression, or clinical data
3. **Get Analysis**: Receive personalized health predictions
4. **View Reports**: Access detailed health reports with explanations

### Data Upload

Upload your data files:
- **Genomic Data**: VCF files
- **Expression Data**: RNA-seq or microarray data
- **Clinical Data**: Lab results, medical history

### Understanding Reports

Health reports include:
- **Risk Scores**: Overall health risk assessment
- **Predictions**: Disease risk and drug response predictions
- **Explanations**: Why predictions were made (SHAP/LIME)
- **Recommendations**: Actionable health recommendations

## For Clinicians

### Patient Management

- View patient profiles
- Access comprehensive health reports
- Review predictions and explanations
- Generate clinical recommendations

### Clinical Decision Support

The platform provides:
- Evidence-based recommendations
- Guideline references
- Risk stratification
- Treatment suggestions

### Reports

Generate reports in multiple formats:
- PDF: For printing and archiving
- JSON: For integration with EHR systems
- HTML: For web viewing

## Features

### Personalized GRN

Build patient-specific gene regulatory networks for advanced analysis.

### Explainable AI

Understand why predictions were made with SHAP and LIME explanations.

### Real-Time Updates

Receive real-time alerts and updates via WebSocket connections.

### Multi-Omics Integration

Combine genomic, expression, and clinical data for comprehensive analysis.
